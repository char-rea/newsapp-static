Why Netball is the Best Team Sport

By Charlotte Hill 22 June 2026

There is something about netball that no other sport can replicate. From the moment you step onto the court, you are part of something bigger than yourself. Every pass, every intercept, every goal scored is a team effort.

Netball teaches you to communicate clearly and trust the people around you. You learn to read the game, anticipate movement, and stay calm under pressure — skills that carry far beyond the court.

Whether you play socially on a Wednesday night or competitively on weekends, netball has a way of bringing people together. The friendships you build on a netball team are some of the strongest you will ever make.

If you have never tried it, find your local club and give it a go. You might just find your favourite hobby.
