A Day Out at the Farm

By Charlotte Hill 18 June 2026

There is nothing quite like a day spent on a farm to reset your mind and reconnect with the simpler things in life. Away from screens and schedules, farm life moves at its own gentle pace — and it is completely refreshing.

One of my favourite things to do is visit a local farm and spend time with the animals. There is something so calming about feeding lambs, watching chickens roam freely, and just breathing in the fresh country air. It sounds simple, but it genuinely lifts your mood.

Farm visits are also a great reminder of where our food actually comes from. Picking fresh vegetables straight from the ground or collecting eggs in the morning gives you a whole new appreciation for the effort that goes into producing the food on our plates every day.

If you ever get the chance to visit a working farm or a pick-your-own fruit farm near you, take it. Pack a bag, wear your gumboots, and enjoy every minute of it.
