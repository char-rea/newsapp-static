My Favourite Vanilla Cupcake Recipe

By Charlotte Hill 20 June 2026

Baking is one of those hobbies that rewards you in the best possible way — with something delicious at the end of it. These vanilla cupcakes are my absolute go-to when I want to bake something simple but impressive.

The recipe itself is straightforward. You will need butter, sugar, eggs, self-raising flour, vanilla extract, and a splash of milk. Cream the butter and sugar together until pale and fluffy, beat in the eggs one at a time, then fold in the flour gently. A teaspoon of vanilla extract makes all the difference.

Bake them at 180°C for around 18 to 20 minutes until they are golden and a skewer comes out clean. The hardest part is waiting for them to cool before you can frost them!

For the frosting, I keep it classic — pink buttercream made with butter, icing sugar, a tiny drop of pink food colouring, and a splash of cream. Pipe it on generously and top with a sprinkle. Simple, pretty, and absolutely delicious every single time.
